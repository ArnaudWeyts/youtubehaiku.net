YouTubeHaiku.net

Enjoy the video posted by the users of the subreddit r/youtubehaiku in a beautiful modern player that will make you waste even more time than usual.

You just need to know that HAIKU are poetic videos under 14 seconds, POETRY are still poetic but longer (up to 30 seconds) and then, you know, there are Memes.

youtubehaiku.net is made by Simone Cingano (aka u/yupswing).
The project is open source and you can fork the code on github

Play!

Videos will play continuosly until the end of the internet. [citation needed]

Control the player with the buttons or use the keyboard shortcuts:

  • or P or SPACE to play/pause
  • or → or N to skip to the next video
  • or ← or B to go back to the previous video
  • or R to replay the current video
  • or C or L to open the reddit post
  • or S or . to open the playlist selection
  • or H or . to show this help box again
